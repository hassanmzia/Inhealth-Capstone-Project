## **Clinical Justification for Additions**

### **Why ECG Agent?**
- **Cardiac patients need continuous electrical monitoring**, not just heart rate
- Detects silent ischemia, arrhythmias that basic wearables miss
- Monitors medication effects (QT prolongation)

### **Why Kidney Agent?**
- **80% of diabetes patients develop kidney disease**
- Kidney function affects medication dosing for both diabetes and cardiac drugs
- Early CKD detection prevents progression

### **Why Pneumonia & COVID Agents?**
- **Diabetes/CVD patients have 3x higher infection risk**
- Pneumonia is leading cause of hospitalization in this population
- COVID-19 has severe outcomes in comorbid patients
- Rural areas need rapid diagnostic capabilities

### **Why Image Quality Agent?**
- **Garbage in, garbage out** - poor images → misdiagnosis
- Rural health workers need guidance on proper acquisition
- Reduces false positives/negatives from Agents 6 & 7
